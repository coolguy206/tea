Tea Gives Back

Tea gives back 10% of profits to Global Fund for Children (GFC) and other groups that support the dignity of children everywhere. We’ve partnered with GFC to support two incredible Brazilian organizations—Cores do Amanhã and Pazear Association—empowering women and children through arts, sports, and education. This March, every donation you make to GFC will be matched by Tea, up to $5,000. Learn more about these organizations and our give back initiatives on our blog.

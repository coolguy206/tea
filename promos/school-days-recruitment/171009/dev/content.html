Tea School Days is open only to registered 501(c)(3) organizations in the U.S. Applications for the Fall 2017 School Days must be received by Tea no later than 11:59 p.m. Pacific Time on 10/02/2017. Tea is not responsible for lost, late, or misdirected applications. Applications will be approved or denied in Tea’s sole discretion. If your application is approved, Tea will contact you and provide you with sample signage you can use to promote School Days to your organization and community. You agree that you will not use the signage, or the Tea name and logo, except to promote your school’s participation in School Days, and you will not list Tea as a sponsor or donor, or use our name and logo in a way that suggests Tea is a school sponsor. To earn 15% off your purchase, 15% donation back to your organization and qualify for free 6-10 business day shipping, orders must be placed between 12:01 am PT on 10/9/2017 and 11:59 pm PT on 10/16/2017. Only orders with your organization’s promo code will be counted towards the final total. Offers cannot be combined with other promotions. No adjustments on previous orders will be made. To receive 15% off your purchase and 15% back to your organization and free (6-10 day) shipping, participants must enter their unique promo code at checkout and click “Apply my promo code”. Free shipping offer is for 6-10 Business Day Shipping only; if 6-10 Business Day Shipping is selected, discount will appear only on the review page of checkout and will show the amount deducted ($7.00) below the order total. With questions about orders, contact our Customer Care team at service@teacollection.com. Tea does not give fundraising updates while School Days is under way, but we will send email to your designated contact within a week after the promotion ends to let you know how much you raised. Checks will be mailed within 3-5 weeks after Tea’s School Days ends. We request that all schools send us an email documenting the receipt of their organization’s check.

FAQs

How do I enroll in the fundraiser?

Click on the 'SIGN UP NOW' button above and enter
your organization's
details. Note: You must be in the U.S.
and certified as a 501(c)(3) organization
to participate. If
you have your Employer
Identification Number (EIN)
handy, then
you're ready to roll!

Don’t have your EIN number on hand?
Click 'SUBMIT A
REFERRAL' to fill out
the basics and we’ll get in touch.

the
fundraiser successful for
my school?

To help get the word out, we’ll provide
you with
branded materials you can
customize and distribute with
your unique
promo code. These include social assets,
email templates, and more!

A shout-out in your school newsletter, a
reminder in an
email blast, and a little
friendly fundraising competition
among
classrooms are some other great ways to
spread
the word!

You can even join our School Days

How does it work?

Once you’re in, we'll reach out before the
fundraiser begins
with a unique promo
code and marketing materials for you to
share with your community, family, and
friends.

During the fundraiser period, shoppers
will shop
teacollection.com and enter the
unique code provided to them
at
checkout to get 20% off their order,
AND at the end Tea
will donate 15% of
your total sales to your school!

For more info on Tea discounts, please visit our promotions page.

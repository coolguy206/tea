Meet Kadzo

Kadzo is the latest Pendo (“love” in
Swahili) doll
from Elimu Girls artisans in
Malindi, Kenya.
You can purchase a
limited edition, hand-made
doll in a wrap
dress in one of six signature Tea
Collection
prints. 100% of your tax-deductible
doll
purchase goes to Elimu Girls.

About Elimu Girls

Elimu Girls provides scholarships,
room and board, and supplies for
young women to attend a two year
vocational sewing program. Graduates
earn a national sewing certificate,
receive ongoing mentorship and their
own sewing machine.

Elimu Artisans

Elimu has hired a small team of alumni to
produce the Kadzo dolls. They receive a
fair
stipend and housing while they build
up their
own customer base and enhance
their income
generating potential. They
receive mentorship
and guidance from
fashion designer at Elimu’s
dedicated
Artisan Maker Space.

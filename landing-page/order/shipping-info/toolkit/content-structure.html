Shipping Info

We currently ship to the 50 United States, Washington, DC and Canada. Recently we have also added international shipping as an option, please see details below. Tea is also available in several international shop locations. Note that our international stores incur fees to import our products so prices may vary from what you see on our site. Delivery times are inclusive of order processing times and are based upon business days.

Additional Notes:

Due to increased costs across the entire supply chain, we have added a $1.95 shipping surcharge to all orders. If you have questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to service@teacollection.com.

Shipping days is inclusive of processing times and days to deliver. All packages will have a status of ‘pending’ until the night that they have been shipped to ensure packages ship complete.

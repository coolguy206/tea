Shipping Info

We currently ship to the 50 United States, Washington, DC and Canada. Recently we have also added international shipping as an option, please see details below. Tea is also available in several international shop locations. Note that our international stores incur fees to import our products so prices may vary from what you see on our site. Delivery times are inclusive of order processing times and are based upon business days.

Additional Notes:

Due to the holiday’s increased costs across the entire supply chain, we have added a 1.95 cent holiday surcharge to all orders through December 2022. If you have questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to service@teacollection.com.

Shipping days is inclusive of processing times and days to deliver. All packages will have a status of ‘pending’ until the night that they have been shipped to ensure packages ship complete.

**Duties, taxes and fees may vary depending on shipping carrier and will be charged either at order placement or shipment depending on payment. Please check the country’s local rates for more information. Note that certain elements, such as going through customs, are out of our control. As such, all times are estimated delivery times.

*** Currently we do not ship to the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea (North), Iran, Russia, and Syria.

We are so excited to be shipping internationally! All the details, below.

Duties and tax estimates will be calculated at the time of placement to ensure we can get the best possible rates for our customers. All shipping cost, including duties and taxes, are real-time estimates and will be finalized at the time of shipment.

Please note, given local and regional carriers, all delivery times are estimates and cannot be guaranteed.

Due to the complexities of international shipping, we will not be able to refund shipping cost or duties & taxes and the customer will be responsible for all return shipping fees.

It’s essential that you include the recipients phone number in the shipping address. Local carriers often use this to communicate with the customer and need this information to fulfill order.

If you have any questions/feedback, please reach out to our customer care team who will be happy to help with your experience. Please send us an email at service@teacollection.com.

Order History & Tracking

To track your package just sign in and click on My Account. In your account you will see order history by date or by order number. You can browse your order history and track the status of your orders. Tracking is real-time and will show information provided by the carrier.

Shipping Info

We currently ship to the 50 United States, Washington, DC and Canada. Tea is also available in several international shop locations. Note that our international stores incur fees to import our products so prices may vary from what you see on our site.

US Shipping Information

Standard Shipping - U.S.

Delivery Time Price Notes
Standard: 6-12 Business Days $7.00 (FREE on orders over $150.00 after any discounts/promotions and pretax) Please select this option when shipping to P.O Boxes and APO/FPO. For APO/FPO addresses, please allow 3 weeks for delivery (will vary depending on location).
Priority: 3-6 Business Days $9.00 Does not deliver to P.O. Boxes and APO/FPO.
Express: 2-3 business days $17.00 Orders placed before 10am will ship out the same day. If the order is placed after 10am it will ship the next business day and arrive within the given time frame.
Overnight: 1 Business Day $25.00 Orders placed before 10am will ship out the same day. If the order is placed after 10am it will ship the next business day and arrive within the given time frame.

Order History & Tracking

To track your package just sign in and click on My Account. In your account you will see order history by date or by order number. You can browse your order history and track the status of your orders. Tracking is real-time and will show information provided by the carrier.

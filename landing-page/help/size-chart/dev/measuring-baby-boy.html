Chest

Wrap a measuring tape under your child's armpits, making sure it passes across the fullest part of his chest and around his shoulder blades. His arms should be relaxed naturally against his sides. (Good luck! You're going to have to be superhero-fast!)

Hips

Have your child stand with his legs together, and wrap the measuring tape in a loop around the widest part of his hips. (If he's not standing yet, have him lie down. You might need a partner to keep him distracted—and still—for a minute!)

Natural Waist

Tie a string around your child's middle and help him bend from side to side. The string will settle around his natural waist, above his hipbones. Wrap a measuring tape around this part of his waist.

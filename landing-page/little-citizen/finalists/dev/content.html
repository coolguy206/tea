Meet 2018's Inspiring
Little Citizens

These five kids show the kindness, compassion and
determination that makes the world a better
place.
And they inspire all of us to be the best citizens we
can be, both in our communities at home
and in
the way we consider our impact on the wider world.

Join us as we donate 5%* of all Tea sales from July
30 - August 5 to help these Little Citizens
deepen
their impact on the causes of their choice.

In 2017, Tea donated $37,258 to support five
finalists' causes across the U.S. $7,451 each
went to:
No Kid Hungry, CASA Sacramento, Make A
Wish Foundation, Ronald McDonald House
and installing
adaptive playground
equipment to a community in need.

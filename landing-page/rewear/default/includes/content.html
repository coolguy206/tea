how do trade ins work?

Trade in your gently used apparel
from Tea Collection and earn $5 off
(per accepted item) toward new items on teacollection.com.*

*$5 off credit will only be given on items that are accepted. Each $5 off code is applicable to one full-priced Tea item, valid on teacollection.com only, not valid in stores.

  1. Gather your gently loved
    Tea Collection items and fill
    out this form to let us know
    what you’re trading in.

    fill out form

  2. Please send all your gently used
    items to the address below.
    Don’t forget to include your name,
    email address and the date submitted
    your trade-in form in your box/bag:
    The Swoondle Society®
    c/o Tea Collection Trade-In
    1 Arnold Drive, Unit 1
    Huntington, NY 11743

  3. For each item accepted, you’ll
    get a special code for $5 off one
    full-priced item at
    teacollection.com. The more
    items accepted, the more codes
    you’ll get!

    shop tea

items we accept

Tea Collection items in new or gently
used condition. There’s no limit on the
number of items you can send!

items we don’t accept

Brands other than Tea Collection,
damaged items, or items that have
stains or excessive wear.

how do trade ins work?

Trade in your gently used apparel
from Tea Collection and earn $5 off
(per accepted item) toward new items on teacollection.com.*

  1. include "images/fill_out_form.html"

    Gather your gently loved
    Tea Collection items and fill
    out this form to let us know
    what you’re trading in.

  2. include "images/mail_or_drop.html"

    Print out your prepaid mailing
    label and send the items to
    Kidizen, OR find a drop-off
    location near you, print out your
    form and bring your items to
    your chosen location.

  3. include "images/earn_rewards.html"

    For each item accepted, you’ll
    get a special code for $10 off one
    full-priced item at
    teacollection.com. The more
    items accepted, the more codes
    you’ll get!

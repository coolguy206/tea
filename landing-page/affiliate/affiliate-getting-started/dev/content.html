Getting Started

Once you've applied for the program and received an approval email, please log in to ShareASale's affiliate gateway and navigate to the "Get Links" area. Next, click on "Get a Link/Banner," find Tea Collection and click "Get Links" next to our company name. You can also visit the Tea Collection affiliate page directly. There you can browse through the different text and banner links available and click "Get this Banner's HTML Code" under each banner and/or link that you want to display. When you add these banners and links to your website, it will be tagged with your unique affiliate ID so that we can give you credit for sales you refer.

Feel free to send us an email with any questions you might have about the program. More info is available on the affiliate program contact page.

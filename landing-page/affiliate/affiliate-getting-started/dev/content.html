Getting Started

Once you've applied for the program (and received an approval email), you’ll need to login to ShareASale’s affiliate gateway to get your custom links. Note: these links will include your unique affiliate ID so that we can give you credit for the sales you refer. Follow these steps:

  1. Go to “LINKS” in the top navigation menu and select “Get a Link/Banner” from the drop-down menu.
  2. Find Tea Collection and click “Get Links” next to our company name. You can browse through the different text and banner links available.
    • Click “Get HTML Code”. From here you can copy the HMTL code or “select URL only” for your custom code/link.
  3. Share your custom banners and link to your platforms and start earning!

Feel free to send us an email with any questions you might have about the program. More info is available on the affiliate program contact page.

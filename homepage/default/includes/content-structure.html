Why Tea Collection

globally inspired

Tea began with the dream to inspire global
connection and curiosity for children. We
travel the world bringing the beauty of
different cultures and modern design to
clothing for babies, toddlers, and kids.

heirloom quality

Our clothes are responsibly made and
designed to last so you’ll be able to pass
your Tea down from kid to kid. We stand
by our quality and have created a
secondhand marketplace called Rewear,
where you can buy, sell, or trade-in
preloved Tea.

we give back

Tea gives back 10% of profits. Since 2002,
we’ve donated over $1.5 million to the
Global Fund for Children and other
organizations to help ensure a better world
for kids everywhere.
